About the project

What is Burnout Radar?

The Nepal–US Hackathon focuses on building practical, impactful solutions that address real-world challenges such as career pressure and burnout among students and professionals.

Burnout Radar is a preventive system designed to detect early signs of burnout using simple daily inputs like sleep, mood, workload, and task deadlines.

It transforms this data into a burnout score, identifies underlying causes, and proactively alerts users when risk increases.

Beyond detection, the system provides actionable recovery plans — reducing workload, taking breaks, guided breathing, and focus sessions.

By combining detection, explanation, and intervention into one feedback loop, Burnout Radar promotes healthier and sustainable productivity.
